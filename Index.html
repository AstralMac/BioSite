Cyd's Accomplishments

Cydney is an amazing mother, a wonderful wife, a superb violinist, and a aspiring film maker! Cydney started playing violin in grade school, having been the "First chair" violinist in her Symphonic Orchestra class. She has traveled around the country to perform, having traveled to places such as Dallas, Tx and Washington DC. Cydney has even performed at Disney land!

Cydney has always had a passion for the film industry. Being a film buff since a young age, she would watch movies with her grandmother every chance she could! Using that passion as motivation Cydney is pursuing her Bachelor's degree in Digital Cinematography at Full Sail University; with plans of building a career as a director in film. But if you were to ask her, her greatest accomplishment is being a mother and having raised an amazing kid.
